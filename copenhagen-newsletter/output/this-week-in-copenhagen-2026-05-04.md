# This Week in Copenhagen — Mon 4–10 May 2026

Welcome to **This Week in Copenhagen** — your Monday-morning rundown of what's worth showing up to between **Mon 4–10 May 2026**. This week: 4 concerts on the books, 1 bigger happening, 3 weekend markets, sport. Sun's up, the city is back outside. Let's go.

## 🎵 Concerts & live music

*When the calendar is good in Copenhagen, it's _good_.*

- **Charlie Parr** — Beta, Amager, Tue 5 May. Minnesota fingerpicker — old-time blues and folk, just him and a guitar. [Tickets](https://beta2300.dk/)
- **L.A. Witch** — Loppen, Christiania, Tue 5 May. Smoky garage rock from the trio out of Los Angeles. [Tickets](https://www.loppen.dk/)
- **Minami Deutsch** — VEGA, Tue 5 May. Tokyo's krautrock-leaning psych quartet, doing the long-form trance thing live. [Tickets](https://vega.dk/en)
- **A Colossal Weekend 2026 (10-year anniversary)** — VEGA + Basement, Vesterbro, Thu–Sat 7–9 May. Three days of post-rock, doom, sludge and heavy experimental. Cult of Luna, Pelican, Russian Circles, King Buffalo, Slift, A.A. Williams, Slow Crush and ~30 more. [Tickets](https://www.acolossalweekend.dk/)

## 🎉 City events & culture

*Bigger things happening in town.*

- **Marina Abramović — "Seven Deaths"** — Cisternerne, Frederiksberg, Tue 5 & Wed 6 May. Cinematic opera installation in the underground reservoir — a rare staging in Copenhagen. [Details](https://frederiksbergmuseerne.dk/en/cisternerne/)

## 🛍️ Markets this weekend

*Saturday and Sunday rummage spots.*

- **Frederiksberg Town Hall flea market** — Smallegade, Frederiksberg, Sat 9 May, 09:00–15:00. Long-running Saturday market on the strip beside the town hall. Easy to combine with a walk in Frederiksberg Have. [Details](https://www.visitcopenhagen.com/copenhagen/activities/shopping/flea-markets-copenhagen)
- **Københavns Loppetorv (Israels Plads)** — Israels Plads, Indre By, Sat 9 May, 11:00–16:00. Open-air flea market right by Nørreport. Books, bric-a-brac, vintage clothing, the occasional weird lamp. [Details](https://kbhloppetorv.dk/)
- **Gentofte loppemarked** — Behind Charlottenlund Station, Sun 10 May, 08:00–14:00. Quiet, suburban Sunday-morning flea market — best in the first hour for finds. [Details](https://www.visitcopenhagen.com/copenhagen/activities/shopping/flea-markets-copenhagen)

## 🍔 Street-food season

*Reasons to eat outdoors this week.*

- **Broens Gadekøkken** — Strandgade 95, by Inderhavnsbroen, open daily this week. The harbour-bridge food market opposite Nyhavn — light evenings, drink-by-the-water energy. [Details](https://broensstreetfood.dk/en/)
- **Reffen street food** — Refshalevej 167, Refshaleøen, open daily this week. Northern Europe's largest street-food market reopens for the season — 35+ stalls, a brewery, harbour view. [Details](https://reffen.dk/en/)

## ⚽ Sport

*Match days and finish lines.*

- **Copenhagen Marathon 2026** — Start at Østre Allé, by Fælledparken, Sun 10 May, gun 09:30. 42.2 km loop through Indre By, Østerbro, Christianshavn, Vesterbro, Nørrebro and Frederiksberg. Best cheering: Dronning Louises Bro turns into a street party with DJs. [Details](https://copenhagenmarathon.dk/en/)
